what are the roles of values empathy and self awareness in learning and programming?

values are important in learning because they will dictate how you learn and how efficient you are. Eempathy and self awareness are important because as a programmer you will be working teams most of the time and having these traits will allow you to fit in to a team better

what has surprised you the most about core learning?

i was surprised how big of a concept core learning was. i thought this would be a relatively shallow concept but it turned out to be really in-depth. i didn't realise emotional intelleigence was a "thing", i didn't know much about neuroplasticity and growth mindset.

what were the most challenging aspects of core learning?

i found the whole foundations quite challenging. i struggled with motivating myself to start working but once i got into it i was able to keep focus. i found understanding neuroplasticty the hardest part of core learning.

because as web developers we constantly work in teams and be able to understand the emotional intelleigence side of things such as how we interact and being aware of ourselves and others feelings are critical to how we interact in a team.
